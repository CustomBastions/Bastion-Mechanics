## Perks
*Animal Handling.* The stable can hold and care for large and smaller beasts. The number of creatures it can hold is based on number the built squares. Each large creature takes 4, medium and small take 1, and tiny takes 1/4. The beasts held in the stable are cared for and well fed.

## Actions
*Acquire Animals.* The stable and acquire a number of beasts (CR 1/8th or less) equal to your proficiency bonus that can be found in the area. The focus will generally be on beasts of burden or riding, like donkeys and horses.
