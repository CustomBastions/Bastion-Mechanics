## Perks
*Study.* By spending at least 4 hours in a day reading in the library, you get the choice between the skills Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature or Religion. You get proficiency or expertise if you are already proficient in the chosen skill. This effect lasts until you finish your second long rest and this can be done once per long rest.
For every additional 1 hours spent in the library until your next long rest you get a +1 to the chosen skill(s) or for every additional 2 hours spent in the library until your next long rest you get to choose an additional skill.

## Actions
*Create Book of Information.* You give your hireling 500 gp and order them to create a Magical item called *Book of Information*. The book is bound to you and if destroyed can be recreated with this action retaining all previously obtained information and it has 2 charge and can't have more than 2 charge and while holding this book you can take a magical action and expend 1 charge. When you do you gain information on target humanoid or beast and the book fills out a page with the information received if no information is gained the book gains one charge and it only expends a charge if the target is a humanoid or beast and has previously not recorded any information on it.


*Recharge Magical Book.* You give your hireling a *Book of Information* and order your hireling to recharge it. The magical item regains 2 charge.

#### Perks
*Creatures and Monster Study.* While doing a Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature and Religion skill check you gain a +3 if it involves any creature or monster this effect is only if you are under the effects of the *Study* perk.

*Create Book of Creatures and Monsters.* You give your hireling 25 gp and order them to create a Magical item called *Book of Creatures and Monsters.* The book has 1 charge and can't have more than 1 charge and while holding this book you can take a magical action and expend 1 charge.
